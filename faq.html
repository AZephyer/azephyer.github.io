Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)?

CBT is a form of psychotherapy that focuses on modifying dysfunctional emotions, behaviors, and thoughts by interrogating and uprooting negative or irrational beliefs. It is a widely accepted form of therapy that provides tools and strategies for problem-solving.

What is Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)?

DBT is a type of cognitive-behavioral therapy that aims to teach people how to live in the moment, develop healthy ways to cope with stress, regulate emotions, and improve relationships with others.

How does 'mediation' differ from life coaching?

What kind of assessments or homework might be included?

Assessments may include questionnaires to understand personal preferences and values, while homework could involve reflective writing, practical implementation of strategies discussed in sessions, or reading assignments to reinforce session themes.

Why do we use Google Docs instead of other client software?

Google Docs is utilized to keep maintenance costs low, thus keeping client costs down as well. It allows for easy communication and accessibility, ensuring that resources are readily available and editable in real-time.
