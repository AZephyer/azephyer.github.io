What I Offer

Postpartum Phase Reflection

This service explores your experiences during the postpartum phase, providing support and strategies for navigating this period.

We focus on emotional well-being, self-care practices, and finding balance during the postpartum period.

Parent & Child Relationships

Strengthen your relationship with your child, improve communication, and address conflicts or challenges.

This service explores parenting strategies that foster a healthy, supportive family dynamic.

Boundary Setting and Maintenance

Setting and maintaining healthy boundaries is crucial for well-being. This service helps you establish and maintain them.

We work together to identify areas where boundaries are needed, and I provide support in maintaining them over time.

Goal Guidance

Whether personal or professional, this service provides the guidance and support needed to achieve your goals.

We focus on setting realistic, achievable goals and creating actionable plans to reach them.

Intimate Relationship Conflict Resolution

This service offers strategies and mediation to resolve conflicts in intimate relationships.

We work on improving communication, rebuilding trust, and finding mutually agreeable solutions to conflicts.

Navigating the "Urban Indigenous Experience"

This service is tailored for those navigating the urban Indigenous experience, exploring identity and community.

We delve into the unique challenges faced by Indigenous individuals in urban settings.
