What I Offer

Parent & Child Relationships

Strengthen your relationship with your child, improve communication, and address conflicts or challenges.

Gain insights into effective parenting techniques that help build a strong and understanding relationship with your child. This service is tailored to assist parents in nurturing a loving and respectful family environment.

Boundary Setting and Maintenance

Setting and maintaining healthy boundaries is crucial for well-being. This service helps you establish and maintain them.

Learn how to effectively set personal and professional boundaries that foster respect and understanding in all your relationships. This guidance is crucial for maintaining your mental health and ensuring healthy interactions.

Goal Guidance

Whether personal or professional, this service provides the guidance and support needed to achieve your goals.

Our goal guidance service helps you define clear, attainable objectives and develop a structured plan to achieve them. This process is tailored to your personal aspirations and professional career goals.

Intimate Relationship Conflict Resolution

This service offers strategies and mediation to resolve conflicts in intimate relationships.

Focus on resolving interpersonal conflicts with effective communication strategies and mediation techniques that promote understanding and reconciliation.

Navigating the "Urban Indigenous Experience"

This service is tailored for those navigating the urban Indigenous experience, exploring identity and community.

Designed to support urban Indigenous individuals, this service helps explore and affirm cultural identity, connect with community resources, and address unique challenges faced in urban settings.
