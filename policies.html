Client Number

Your Client Number is essential for payment methods and record-keeping. Ensure that all correspondence includes your Client Number to avoid any discrepancies.

Cancellation and Session Rescheduling

If you need to cancel or reschedule a session, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. Cancellations or rescheduling requests made less than 24 hours before the scheduled session may incur a fee equivalent to the session cost.

Session Completion

Each session is designed to last 1 hour and 15 minutes. This duration includes a review of the previous session, the core mediation process, and planning for the upcoming week.

Session Cancellation (Early-End)

Both you and the mediator reserve the right to end a session early if necessary. Any remaining time from the session will either be credited towards a future session or refunded, depending on the circumstances.

Confidentiality Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance. All sessions are confidential and comply with provincial laws regarding confidentiality and privacy. No session information will be shared without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

Remote Sessions

All sessions are conducted remotely via Zoom. Documents will be shared through Google Docs and Google Drive to facilitate easy collaboration. The primary method of correspondence will be email, though phone calls can be used if preferred. Please inform the mediator of any accommodations needed to facilitate your participation in remote sessions.

Session Recordings

Session recordings are not typically made to preserve the confidentiality of our sessions. However, if a recording is necessary for any reason, it will only be done with your prior consent, and the recording will be securely stored and deleted after its intended use.

Payment Policy

All payments are to be made via e-transfer to introspectivemediator@gmail.com. Please ensure that you use your Client Number as the password for the e-transfer to avoid any payment issues. Payments must be received prior to the scheduled session.

Refund Policy

Refunds are available for any sessions canceled with more than 24 hours' notice. If a session is canceled with less than 24 hours' notice, refunds are typically not provided unless under exceptional circumstances. In the event that the mediator cancels a session, a full refund or rescheduled session will be offered.
