My Pricing Structure

At The Introspective Mediator, I strive to make my services accessible to everyone. My pricing is designed to accommodate various financial situations while maintaining the quality of service. The low-income rates are based on an honor system and are voluntary, meaning I trust you to select the rate that best reflects your financial situation. Please note that no documentation is required to qualify for the low-income rate.

Postpartum Support Sessions

If you're booking a Postpartum Support session, you can do so directly without the need for an initial consultation. These sessions are designed to offer immediate support and can be booked at your convenience. Please use the scheduling page to book these sessions directly.

Payment Methods

Payments are accepted via e-transfer to introspectivemediator@gmail.com. Please use your Individual Client Number as the password to avoid payment discrepancies.

Discounts for Multi-Session Packages

When you purchase a multi-session package, you benefit from a reduced per-session rate. This is an excellent option if you plan to engage in ongoing mediation.
